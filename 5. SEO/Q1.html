1. A What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?

Ans. Search engine Optimization(SEO) is a strategy or a set of practices aimed at improving a website's visibility and ranking on search engine result pages. The primary goal is to increase organic traffic to a website by optimizing various elements of site and its content.

Importance of SEO

  • Increased visibility
  • Cost optimization
  • Better user experience
  • Targeted traffic
  • Competitive advantage
